See also|Kumkum (TV series)|Vermilion Kumkuma  is a powder used for social and religious markings in India. 
It is either made from turmeric or any other local materials. 
The turmeric is dried and powdered with a bit of slaked lime, which turns the rich yellow powder into a red color.
