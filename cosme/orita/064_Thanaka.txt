Thanaka (MYname|MY=သနပ်ခါး|MLCTS=sa. nap hka:; IPA-my|θənəkʰá|pron, also spelt thanakha) is a yellowish-white Cosmetics|cosmetic paste made from ground b. 
It is a distinctive feature of the culture of Burma, seen commonly applied to the face and sometimes the arms of women and girls, and is used to a lesser extent also by men and boys.
