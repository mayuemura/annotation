An antifungal medication is a pharmaceutical drug|pharmaceutical fungicide used to treat and prevent mycosis|mycoses such as athlete's foot, ringworm, candidiasis (thrush), serious systemic infections such as Cryptococcus (fungus)|cryptococcal meningitis, and others. Such drugs are usually obtained by a doctor's Prescription drug|prescription, but a few are available OTC (Over-the-counter drugs|over-the-counter).
