Powder puffs are pieces of soft material used for the application of face powder. 
They may be shaped as balls or pads.
Historically, powder puffs have been made of very fine down feathers, cotton, fine Wool|fleece, etc. 
In modern times synthetic materials are widely used for powder puffs.
In addition to softness, an important characteristic of powder puffs is their intake ability, i.e., the ability to hold powder.
