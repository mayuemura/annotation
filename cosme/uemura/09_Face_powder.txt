Face powder is a cosmetics|cosmetic Powder (substance)|powder applied to the face to set a foundation (cosmetics)|foundation after application. 
It can also be reapplied throughout the day to minimize shininess caused by oily skin. 
There is translucent sheer powder, and there is pigmented powder. 
Certain types of pigmented facial powders are meant be worn alone with no base foundation. 
Powder tones the face and gives an even appearance.
